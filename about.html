About GiveDirectly

We give cash directly to people living in poverty. GiveDirectly is a nonprofit that lets donors like you send money directly to the world's poorest households. In doing so, we aim to accelerate the end of extreme poverty globally.

Giving cash is a direct, dignified, and proven way to reduce poverty

Since 2017, GiveDirectly has delivered more than $300 million in unconditional cash transfers to over 227,000 low-income Americans and proven that cash can have positive impacts for people in need.
